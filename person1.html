Chun-Le Guo (郭春乐)

Lecture @ Nankai University

Chun-Le Guo is a Lecturer at the College of Computer Science, Nankai University, China. He received his PhD degree from Tianjin University in 2020. He conducted the Ph.D. research as a Visiting Student with the School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science, Queen Mary University of London (QMUL), UK. Then He continued his research as a Research Associate with the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU of HK).

His research interests include computer vision and deep learning, particularly in the domain of image and video restoration and enhancement. His research works (Zero-DCE and Zero-DCE++) on low-light image enhancement have significant impacts on industry and academia. He published over 20 journal and conference papers and obtained over 2,000 Google scholar citations. 2 papers were selected as ESI Highly Cited Paper. 1 paper was selected as ESI Hot Paper.
