π: Photography & Imaging

Research Group

π Research Group @ Nankai University
π was formed in 2023, with a research focus on low-level vision. The group works closely with MMLab@NTU and TMCC@Nankai. The foundation of π can be traced back to the collaboration between Dr. Chongyi and Dr. Chunle during their time as students at Tianjin University, where they experienced memorable moments. π is a new research group and is always looking for motivated Master students, PhD students, postdocs, and research assistants. We are open to collaborating with academia and industry.
