Achievement

Champion and Second Runner-up for Blind Denoising Challenge

Our teams Feedback and Feedforward won the champion (1/317) and the second runner-up (2/317) in MegCup Efficient Model for Blind Denoising

Paper Prize Award 2023 - Application

Our paper 'Bayesian Retinex Underwater Image Enhancement' has been recognized as Paper Prize Award 2023-Application by Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence.

The Journal of Engineering Applications of AI Paper Prize is awarded for outstanding papers on theory or practice of artificial intelligence published in Journal of Engineering Applications of AI. At each IFAC World Congress the prize is awarded to the authors of two selected papers published in the journal in the three years preceding the Congress.

Best Paper Honourable Mention

Our paper 'Underwater Scene Prior Inspired Deep Underwater Image and Video Enhancement' has been recognized as Best Paper Honourable Mention by Pattern Recognition.
